Who we are

The story of Servercom began with a shared vision — to simplify and modernize the way people and businesses move money across the world. Our founders, fintech innovators and financial strategists, recognized a growing need for faster, more secure, and more transparent digital payments. What started as a small team passionate about transforming finance has evolved into a global platform trusted by thousands for seamless transactions, intelligent investments, and financial freedom. At Servercom, we believe in a borderless financial world where payments are instant, secure, and empowering. We’re not just building a payment system — we’re shaping the future of digital finance.

History

  • 2019 (April) – Servercom is founded with the goal of revolutionizing payment systems.
  • 2020 (January) – Launch of our first global payment gateway supporting multiple currencies.
  • 2021 (September) – Servercom surpasses 100,000 users and expands into over 50 countries.
  • 2022 (March) – Introduction of real-time transaction analytics and fraud detection AI.
  • 2023 (July) – Servercom earns full regulatory compliance certification as a trusted fintech provider.
  • 2024 (November) – Launch of the Servercom Business Suite, empowering enterprises with advanced payment automation.
  • 2025 (Present) – Trusted by millions of users, Servercom continues to redefine global finance — one secure transaction at a time.
